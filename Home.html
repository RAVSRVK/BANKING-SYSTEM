The Sparks bank

Sparks bank is a medium-sized public sector bank (PSB), with a network of 2000 branches and 1563 automated teller machines (ATMs) as on 30 Nov 2013. The Government of India owns 58% of its share capital and is going to increase it to 62.14% by infusing INR2 billion (US$32 million) in capital.A total business of INR 2230 billion (US$36 billion) for the fiscal year ended 31 March 2013.
